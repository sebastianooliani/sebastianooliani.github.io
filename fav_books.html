Favourite Books

Here are some of my favourite books. I have read more than 50 books just in the last 5 years. These can be considered as the ones which left a mark on me. I have read them in different periods of my life. For example, I read Odyssey and watched the entire series from Franco Rossi when I was 12.
I mostly read in Italian and do not have a favourite genre. I listed the english title next to the italian one, but I read all the following in Italian. I know that I should read more in English (since I used it most of my days), but I find it easier and more relaxing to read in my native language.
I read on average more than 10 books per year. I would like to read more, but I have a lot of things to do.
Nevertheless, I am satisfied with my reading habits.
