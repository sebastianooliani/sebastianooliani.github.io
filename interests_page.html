Interests

Besides my studies, in my free-time I love listening and playing music, reading books or doing outdoor activities.

I have been playing football for many years, especially during high school. Now, I am playing it less often, but I still enjoy it. Right now, during my weeks I like riding my bike and swimming, which I find more relaxing and less stressful. I also enjoy hiking in the mountains, but I have not done it too much.

During high school, I played electric guitar and bass guitar. I took lectures of both of them until when I moved to Bologna for my bachelor. I have played in several small concerts and I also recorded two songs (Sweet Dreams by the Eurythmics and Chandelier by Sia) in a studio together with a modern music orchestra. I recorded the guitar parts for both songs. Unfortunately, I cannot find these recordings anymore, but they are definitely somewhere online.

Although it might sound strange, I do not have a favourite music genre, but I listen to a lot of different things, either old or new. I grow up listening mostly to rock music, and that's why I love to play the guitar and the bass, but I am actually listening to very different things. From hard rock to slow ballads, from jazz to rap, and from rap to disco music. I love to discover new music and artists. You can read the list of my favourite albums I have listen to.
